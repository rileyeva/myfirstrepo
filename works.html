In relation to the pdf you can download, here are some descriptions of Riley’s works through the years, from painting, to tattooing, illustrations and finally a few graphic design concept projects.

Paintings

Photos 1 through 3 are a series of flower paintings Riley created over a 6 month period. By using bright colours and painting what she knows best, Riley was able to use art to work through tough situations and grow her style.

Tattoos

Photos 4 through 6 are tattoo projects that Riley felt best portrayed her work as a tattoo artist. She loved using linework, dotwork and small fill work with organic shapes to produce fun and interesting tattoos.

Illustrations

Photo 7 was a project Riley created for an art submission for a zine on Sonastalgia, or “eco-anxiety.” Her work was chosen among a few other artists to be featured in the zine.

Photos 8 through 10 are illustrations Riley created on her own time using procreate. Number 9 is part of a 5 part series of illustrations she sold as prints at two art markets.

Graphic Design

Finally, photos 11 through 13 are works Riley created for school projects. Number 11 is a street banner concept for Granville Island created on procreate, number 12 is a magazine concept created on photoshop and number 13 is a poster created on illustrator, representing the cherry blossom festivals in Japan.
